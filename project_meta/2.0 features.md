# CityPulse v2.0 Features

## Core Gameplay
- **Swipe Mechanic**: Tinder-style Left/Right decision making.
- **Resource Management**: Balancing Land, Budget, Health, and Happiness.
- **Dynamic Deck System**: 15-card sessions drawn from a larger pool.

## New in v2.0
### Scenarios
- **Standard**: Balanced gameplay.
- **Silver Tsunami**: Ageing population crisis (Low Health start).
- **Climate Siege**: Environmental crisis (Low Budget start, high stakes).

### Feedback Bonus
- **Citizen Voice**: 10% chance for players to input text feedback for bonus rewards.

### Gamified Review
- **Grading**: S to F ranking based on adherence to DMP2025 principles.
- **Analysis**: Highlights specific mistakes and great moves with educational context.

### Content Expansion
- **Full DMP2025 Card Set**: Includes Long Island, Turf City, JLD, and more.
- **Bad Cards**: Explicit "Failure" cards (Gated Enclave, Concrete Canal) to test player knowledge.
