# City Planning Card Deck - Card List (v2.0)

This document lists all available cards in the CityPulse Deck, including their strategic rationale and effect on game resources.

## Card Types
- **Choice**: Standard decision card with Yes/No trade-offs.
- **Press Conference**: A roleplay event where the player answers a question (No resource change, but potential bonus).
- **Mega-Project**: High-impact card with massive costs but critical long-term benefits.

## Resources
- **Budget**: Financial reserves.
- **Land**: Available space / efficiency.
- **Health**: Public health and environmental resilience.
- **Happiness**: Social cohesion and public approval.

---

## Card Index

### 1. 10-Minute Neighbourhood
- **Type**: Choice
- **Description**: Reorganize urban space so residents can access amenities within a 10-minute walk.
- **Strategic Value**: Reduces traffic, activates precincts 24/7. Key to "Happy, Healthy City".
- **Impact (Yes)**: Budget (+15), Land (+20), Health (+25), Happiness (+30)
- **Impact (No)**: Minor negative impacts across board.

### 2. Turf City Redevelopment
- **Type**: Choice
- **Description**: Develop high-density public housing in the prestigious Bukit Timah district.
- **Strategic Value**: Mitigates stratification and prevents "rich ghettos".
- **Impact (Yes)**: Budget (+10), Land (+25), Health (-10), Happiness (+40)

### 3. Gated Enclave (BAD CARD)
- **Type**: Failure Card
- **Description**: Approve a private, walled community.
- **Strategic Value**: **AVOID**. Destroys social cohesion.
- **Impact (Yes)**: Land (-20), Happiness (-30)

### 4. JLD Gateway Strategy
